About me

Hi, thanks for visiting my portfolio!

Currently, I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Engineering at the University of Cagliari. Since December 2019, I have been collaborating with the Astrolavos Lab (Georgia Institute of Technology, Georgia, USA) on a project about malicious behavior detection and classification in the Internet of Things. My main activity fields are Cybersecurity and Software Engineering.

I have always been drawn to Computer Science and technologies. That's why I decided to start my academic education with a bachelor degree in Computer Engineering. After that, I enrolled in a master degree course in Internet Engineering to complete the training I have always wanted. The academic career allowed me to study and work on a wide variety of applications in the field of Internet of Things, Machine Learning, Cyber Security, Web Development, and Software Engineering.

Thanks to my experiences as a graduate student and a Ph.D. student, I am a fast-learning and a technology enthusiastic person. I enjoy the possibility to organize and exchange ideas to come up with an approach to achieve the most significant outcomes for the team.

For the future, I am looking for a position in the information security field in which I can improve my technical and interpersonal skills working on impactful and stimulating projects.

Please, feel free to explore my projects and education.
