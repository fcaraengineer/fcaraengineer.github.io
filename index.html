About me

Hi, thanks for visiting my portfolio!

A fast-learning and technology enthusiastic person, I completed my academic studies in Computer Engineering and Telecommunications with honors. Then, I received a Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering, where I had the possibility to specialize in Cybersecurity and malware analysis.

During my time as a researcher, I primarily focused on the design of static, dynamic, and machine learning-based analyzers for malicious applications that usually employed code obfuscation or adversarial machine learning. I also had the possibility of working for six months in the Astrolavos Lab at Georgia Tech (Atlanta, Georgia, USA) as a Ph.D. researcher. During this experience, I conducted research on Internet of Things device security and Machine Learning.

Currently, I am a Consultant in Cloud & Infrastructure Security at Avanade. My focus is on improving the security posture of cloud applications and designing secure systems and processes for cloud infrastructures. I mainly work with Microsoft Azure and all its services, but I also have the possibility to work with Amazon Web Services.
Moreover, I continuously seek new learning opportunities through certifications and on-the-job experience to improve my knowledge and skills.
